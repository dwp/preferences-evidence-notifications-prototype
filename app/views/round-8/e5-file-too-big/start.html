Send your evidence

On this service, you can send us evidence when you have been asked to do so by the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP).

What benefits can I upload evidence for?

You can currently only upload evidence for Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) on this service.

What types of evidence can I send?

You can send us evidence of:

  • your bank statements
  • your payslips
  • your P60s

Uploading your files

You will be able to upload multiple files for your evidence, such as for a bank statement that has multiple pages.

Your files must:

  • be JPG, JPEG, HEIC, HEIF, BMP, PNG, TIF or PDF
  • be smaller than 50MB
  • not be password protected

You will be able to upload a maximum of 100MB in total.
