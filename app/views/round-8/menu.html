E5

This prototype incorporates a series of iterations based on UR feedback, insights and recent changes in technical requirements.

Testing scheduled with 8 customers starting 28th October 2024.

UR testing scenarios

Scenario 1: Happy path.

  • run through happy path
  • what would they do if they needed to upload another doc

Scenario 2: File too big.

Unique prototype
  • run through
  • what do they do
  • how do they handle this problem, for example would they give up and post their evidence, compress their files and/or take another picture of lower quality

Scenario 3: Upload failed.

Unique prototype
  • double run through; first file upload fails, second one is successful
  • how would they react to the loading spinner rejecting their first attempt
  • would they try again?
  • would they make any changes?

Scenario 4: 12 bank statements.

  • run through
  • what would they do if they hit the file limit after the 12th file? Would they upload the 12th file one in a new journey?
  • what would they do if they needed to upload another piece of evidence?

Scenario 5: Remove a file, when only one file has been uploaded.

  • run through
  • do they understand what to do?

Bonus scenarios (if time allows)

Scenario 6: Provide customer with a council tax letter as evidence.

  • do they understand what to do?
  • do they understand that the service currently only works for bank statements, payslips and P60s?

Scenario 7: Provide customer with a letter from PIP, not ESA.

  • do they realise that the service is only for Employment Support Allowance (ESA)?
  • do they understand what to do?
  • do they continue with the journey anyway?
