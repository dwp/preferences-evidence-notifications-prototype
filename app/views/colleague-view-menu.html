Iteration 5.0

New interaction designer to continue adding new scenarios to this design.

Name Description Status Action
5.0 This journey includes scenarios for people who get benefits, State Pension, and both benefits and State Pension. It also includes scenarios for people who get paid via the Payment Exception Service, and signposting to help users update their bank details another way. design in progress View

Iteration 5.1

This is a partial journey of iteration 5.0. Designs include the happy path only for people who get benefits for user testing round 15.

We're conducting an A/B test to understand what users think, feel and do about the 'are you expecting a payment in the next 6 working days?' question in the eligibility journey.

Name Description Status Action
5.1A This journey includes the question, 'are you expecting a payment in the next 6 working days?' Depending how the user answers, the content will change later in the journey to explain whether their payment will likely go into their new account, or the one on our records now (before the change). design in progress View
5.1B We've removed the question, 'are you expecting a payment in the next 6 working days?' Without this question, this journey only includes the path for people who do not have a payment due and the content explains that their next payment will most likely go into their new account. design in progress View
