Cookies

Cookies are small files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to make our services work and collect information about how you use our service.

Essential cookies

Essential cookies keep your information secure while you use our services. We do not need to ask permission to use them.

Name Purpose Expires

SESSIONID Keeps you signed in while you are using the service This session only

Cookies that measure website use (Google Analytics)

With your permission, we use Google Analytics to collect data about how you use our services. This information helps us to improve our service.

Google is not allowed to use or share our analytics data with anyone.

Google Analytics stores anonymised information about:

  • how you got to our service
  • the pages you visit on our services and how long you spend on them
  • any errors you see while using our service
