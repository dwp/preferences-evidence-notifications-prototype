Check if you can change your bank details using this service

Before you sign in, you need to answer questions to check if you can change your bank details for your benefits online.

If you’re using this service for the first time

You will need to register if you have not used this service before.

To register, you’ll need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone
  • your National Insurance number

We’ll ask you about 2 types of identification to help protect you from fraud.

You may be asked about:

  • valid UK passport
  • payslips or P60
  • Self Assessment
  • information about tax credits
  • details of your bank accounts, loans, mortgages or credit agreements

If you have used this service before

You’ll need the email address, mobile phone and password you used to register.
