Before you start

You will need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone
  • your National Insurance number

We will send security codes to your email and phone to check they belong to you.

You will also need information from 2 sources so we can confirm your identity and help protect you from fraud.

You can choose from:

  • a valid UK passport
  • UK driving licence
  • payslips or P60
  • Self Assessment
  • information about tax credits, if you claim them
  • details of your bank accounts, loans, mortgages or credit agreements
