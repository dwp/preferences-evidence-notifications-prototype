About your Self Assessment

Enter the pensions and benefits information from your tax return

  • Use a Self Assessment tax return from 2018 to 2019, or 2019 to 2020
  • Enter the exact amount, for example, 923
  • If a pension or benefit does not apply to you, enter 0
Where to find these details on your tax return

Enter the information from your tax return using the numbered boxes to help you.

For example, on your tax return:

  • State Pension is box 8
  • Pensions (other than State Pensions), retirement annuities and taxable lump sums treated as pensions is box 11
  • Taxable Incapacity Benefit and contribution-based Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) is box 13
  • Jobseeker’s allowance is box 15
  • Total of any other taxable State Pensions and benefits is box 16
