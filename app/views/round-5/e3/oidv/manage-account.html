Manage your sign in details

You can change the details you use to sign in. You will need to sign in again if you change these.

  • Change your password
  • Change your mobile phone number
  • Change your email address

Updating your contact details

Any changes you make will only update your sign in details. If you want to change your contact details call the Department for Work and Pensions on 0800 123 456.
